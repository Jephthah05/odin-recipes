Kraby Patty

Description

The Krabby Patty is made out of a frozen meatless burger with buns, the patty, pickles, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, ketchup, mustard, and onions and with other elements according to a Krabby Patty secret formula.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed buns
  • Cheese
  • Imitation crab meat patty
  • Sea ketchup
  • Sea mustard
  • Sea lettuce
  • Sea tomato
  • Sea pickles
  • Sea Onion

Steps

  1. Combine crab meat with ingredients in a bowl
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and bake for 10 minutes
